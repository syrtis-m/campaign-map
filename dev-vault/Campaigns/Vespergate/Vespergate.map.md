Vespergate — a large walled harbor city (procgen tuning demo, plan 019 era).
1 unit = 50 m, so the city spans ~4.8 x 3.6 km — 8x6 generation tiles at full
city tier. The bay eats the east edge; the River Vesper runs NW->bay with the
Ashwash joining from the SW; Old Town sits inside the wall with gates at the
cardinal roads; Harborside crowds the docks. All water/wall/road/district/park
geometry is sketched fabric, so every generated street is constraint-shaped.
