Kanto (test overworld) — a Pokémon-style region sketched with the fabric tools to
exercise the zoom/LOD system at a realistic human scale. 1 map unit = 150 m, so the
region is ~2.4 × 2.85 km; a person (1.7 m) is actual-size when you zoom to street
level. Sea to the west, Pallet Town (south) linked by Route 1 to Viridian City, then
Route 2 up into Viridian Forest; tall-grass patches (parks), a river, and mountain
ledges (walls) round it out.
